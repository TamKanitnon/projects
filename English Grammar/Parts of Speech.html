Nouns

1. Common Nouns and Proper Nouns (and Pronoun)

  1. Common nouns are:
    In my class at Oxford University, I have many friends. My best friend is Jan.
    I have a lot of love for her. Jan has a cute dog. Its name is Juju.

  2. Proper nouns are:
    In my class at Oxford University, I have many friends. My best friend is Jan.
    I have a lot of love for her. Jan has a cute dog. Its name is Juju.

  3. Pronoun are:
    In my class at Oxford University, I have many friends. My best friend is Jan.
    I have a lot of love for her. Jan has a cute dog. Its name is Juju.

    I want to drink some coldwater.

  4. Let's meet at the bus stop.

  5. I have two alarms clock.

  5. Abstract Ideas: time, information, love, beauty

  6. Observation:
    It is a broken cup.

  7. Material:

    It is a coffee cup.

Adverbs

1. Position of Adverb in sentence.

  1. She climbed the mountain slowly. (After the sentence)
    Slowly she climbed the mountain. (Before the sentence)
    She slowly climbed the mountain. (Before main verb)

  2. I will download the file tomorrow. (After the sentence)
    Tomorrow I will download the file. (Before the sentence)
    I will tomorrow download the file. (Before main verb)

2. Example Adverb

  1. He speaks nicely.
  2. He runs quickly.
  3. The car drove fastly fast. (เพราะ fast เป็นทั้งคำคุณศัพท์และคำกริยาวิเศษณ์)
